oSTEM

I was the Event Coordinator for oSTEM. oSTEM is the national professional and collegiate organisation for queer people in stem.
We work to provide professional development opportunities, solidarity, and community for LGBTQ+ workers in industry and academic settings. The Drexel chapter was started by Charlie Rose Stuart but folded due to the pandemic, with his help we have revitalised the organisation We have participated in Philly Code Fest, consulting with the CCI DEI Council, a professional headshots event with affirming workplace attire and makeup, serving as a guide for queer people in the workplace to feel comfortable with themselves.
